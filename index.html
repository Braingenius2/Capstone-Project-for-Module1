"Hello! Chess wunderkinds"
FIDE WORLD CHESS TEEN TOURNAMENT 2023

The historical pinnacle of the FIDE teen tournament decides the two champions to contend for the champions crown. Joins us from Astana, Kazakhstan.

2015.04.25 (TUES) ~ 28 (FRI) @ Chess hotels, Astana, Kazakhstan

Main Program

  • Games

    The contenders go head to head in a round robin tournament format.

  • Exhibition

    Simultaneous Exhibition with GM Daniil Dubov, followed by autograph and photo session.

  • Press Conference

    The conference will include insightful discussions on strategy and predictions for the tournament, as well as personal anecdotes and insights into the players' lives off the board

  • Skills Workshop

    This will be packed with some great stategies and tricks of the game by the World champion!

  • Career

    Showcase of some possible career paths as a professional chess player.

Featured Speakers

  • Ding Liren

    Grandmaster. World chess championship Contender

    highest rated Chinese chess player in history and is also a three-time Chinese Chess Champion.

  • Hikaru Nakamura

    Twitch streamer. Grandmaster. Reigning World Fischer Random Chess Champion.

    He earned his grandmaster title at the age of 15, the youngest American at the time to do so. With a peak rating of 2816, Nakamura is the tenth-highest rated player in history.

  • Magnus Carlsen

    Grandmaster. Greatest Chess Player of the century

    Carlsen has held the number 1 position in the FIDE world chess rankings since 1 July 2011 and trails only Garry Kasparov in time spent as the highest-rated player in the world. His peak rating of 2882 is the highest in history. He also holds the record for the longest unbeaten streak at the elite level in classical chess.

  • Daniel Naroditsky

    Grandmaster

    He is an American chess grandmaster, author, and commentator. He published his first chess book at age 14

  • Daniel Rensch

    International master

    He holds the Arizona state record for youngest national master, at the age of 14.[1] He is the president of American Chess Events LLC[2] and Chief Chess Officer of Chess.com.

  • Gary Kasparov

    Grandmaster. Longest reigning World champion of recent times

    He is Russian chess grandmaster, former World Chess Champion (1985 - 2000), political activist and writer. His peak FIDE chess rating of 2851,[3] achieved in 1999, was the highest recorded until being surpassed by Magnus Carlsen in 2013.
